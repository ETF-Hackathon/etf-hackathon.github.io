About Us

We are the hackathon division of a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization called Elevate the Future. Elevate the Future is an organization focused on providing youth with the resources and support in order to spark their passions and set them up for success. Within the hackathon division, we hope to help students on gaining insightful understandings of the technology while promoting them to use their creativity to create monumental innovations and inventions.
