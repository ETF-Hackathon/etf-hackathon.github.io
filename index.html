About Us

Elevate the Future Hackathons is an initiative fiscally sponsored by 501(c)(3) nonprofit Elevate the Future. We aimed to inspire youths to engage and spark their passions in STEM fields by hosting hackathons and events. These events will allow students to gain insightful understandings of the technology while promoting them to use their creativity to create monumental innovations and inventions.
